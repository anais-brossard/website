MY PROJECT

My name is Anais Brossard, and I am a student at Kedge Business school, in the "Programme Grandes Ecoles". I have created this webpage, because I would like to develop an ecocup that could detect the presence of GHB. The drinks would change of color as soon as a drug is put in the glass. GOALS

The term date rape drug refers to certain products that can be added to a drink without changing the taste, appearance or color. This is how some dishonest people aim to make a person absorb the product and then abuse him.

Each year, it is estimated that millions of men and women will unwittingly consume a "date rape drug" unknowingly slipped into their drink. These drugs, often slipped into alcoholic beverages, can cause amnesia, and blackouts, and they're incredibly difficult to detect.

This is why I would like to develop ecocups that would immediatly change color to warn you when someone slips a date rape drug into your drink. This glass will be available in bars, festivals and nightclubs. Moreover, I would like to organize an awarness campaign to sensitize the population and try to do partnership with associations like RAINN (Rape, Abuse & Incest National Network).
